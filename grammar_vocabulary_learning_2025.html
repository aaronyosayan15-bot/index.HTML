Grammar Lessons

1. Nouns

A noun is a person, place, thing, or idea. Example: dog, city, happiness.

2. Verbs

A verb describes an action or state. Example: run, is, eat.

3. Adjectives

An adjective describes a noun. Example: beautiful, tall, happy.
